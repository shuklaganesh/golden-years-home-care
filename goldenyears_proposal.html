Root Cause — The Platform

  • Built on Lovable — an AI builder producing a JavaScript SPA that Google cannot fully crawl
  • No sitemap — confirmed 404 at /sitemap.xml
  • Homepage duplicated across www and non-www versions
  • No per-page SEO controls — title tags, schema markup inaccessible

What It Costs Daily

  • Families searching “home care Addison IL” find competitors, not Golden Years
  • Competitor ranks above in Google Maps with 9× more reviews
  • “Learn More” on every service links to Contact page — visitors leave before enquiring
  • All leads currently come from word-of-mouth alone
Current GBP — 1 review, no hours, no photos. Competitor ranks above.
Golden Years Home Care LLC — 1 review vs competitor’s 9. “Add hours” prompt confirms the listing is incomplete. This is the first impression families see.
The good news: every one of these problems is fixable. None require starting from scratch on the brand or the business — only on the platform and content architecture. That is precisely what this proposal addresses.

Scope of Work

What we will deliver — and why each piece matters
02 / 05

Phase 1 — Website Rebuild on WordPress (One-time)

Migration from Lovable to WordPress + Elementor — the platform that gives Google everything it needs to find, crawl, and rank the site. Built with the full SEO foundation in place from day one.

Platform Migration

Full rebuild on WordPress + Elementor. Existing design language and brand colours preserved. Lovable dependency eliminated entirely.

Service Pages (5–6 pages)

Individual page per service — Companion Care, Personal Care, Dementia Care, 24-Hour Care, Post-Surgery Care. Each with unique content and targeted keywords.

Location Pages (6–8 pages)

Dedicated page per city served in Northern Illinois — Addison, Elmhurst, Carol Stream, and others. Each optimised for local search queries.

SEO Foundation

Unique title tags and meta descriptions per page. LocalBusiness schema markup. XML sitemap submitted to Google Search Console. Canonical URL configured. www/non-www duplicate resolved.

User Journey Fix

“Learn More” navigates to service detail pages. Clear CTA on every page driving to Free Assessment booking. Click-to-call phone number above the fold on mobile.

Trust & Social Proof

Client testimonials, Illinois Home Services Agency licence display, Google Reviews widget, and caregiver team section added to homepage and key pages.

Phase 1 — Google Business Profile Setup (Included — starts immediately)

  • Complete all missing GBP fields — hours, service categories, service area, business description
  • Upload photos — office, caregivers (with permission), and branded imagery
  • Link website and ensure NAP consistency across GBP and the new site
  • Set up a review generation process — templated outreach to current and past clients
  • Submit business to key home care directories — Caring.com, A Place for Mom, HomeAdvisor
Delivery timeline: 6–8 weeks from kickoff to go-live. Weekly check-ins at each milestone. Unlimited design revisions through the design phase. 30-day post-launch support included.
